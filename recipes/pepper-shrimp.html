Pepper Shrimp

I was first introduced to pepper shrimp 2 years ago at a local chinese resturant. From that day on I fell in love with the dish. It times for me to share my recipe for making it

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. medium-size or large shrimp in shells
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 1/2 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp. minced ginger
  • 1 tsp. fresh chopped hot pepper or 1 tsp. hot chilli pepper
  • 1/2 cup finely sliced chive or green onions

Sause

  • 1 tbsp. Worcestershire sause
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 2 tbsp. tomatoes paste or ketchup
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tsp. suger
  • 1 1/2 tsp. cornflour
    •

Method

  1. Cut shrimp through back of shell with kitchen scissors and remove vein.
  2. Sprinkle shrimp with salt and rub in, leave for 15 minutes. Rince well and pat dry.
  3. Heat 1 1/2 tablespoons oil in pan. add shrimp and stir-fry until shrimp is cooked thoroughly; remove from pan.
  4. Heat rest of oil, add garlic, ginger and hot pepper, stir-fry for 5 seconds.
  5. Blend all ingredients for cooking sause together.
  6. Return shrimp to pan and add chive and cooking sause; cook stirring for a few seconds until sause bubbles and thickens.
